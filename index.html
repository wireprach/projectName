POWER LINES

One of the most basic necessities in modern society is electricity. It powers our homes, workplaces, and provides a means for us to access easy communication, and convenient access to the internet. This access has evolved over time. Power lines have long been the site for advancing internet and broadband transmission technology, and with continual technological advancements, this technology has since been replaced with faster and more efficient wireless networks.

ANTENNAS

As another form of technology that functions by sending and receiving signals, antennas are often used for connecting televisions and phones. This connection has also evolved with the advancement of the internet. As entertainment and network streaming services continue to increase in number, antennas over time, have also become a means for people to interact and become connected through the internet.

CAMERAS

Within the last decade CCTV cameras have become more incresingly present in modern society. Commonly seen in private, public and industrial settings, cameras have long been used as a means to safeguard and protect. However, as of the last couple of years, society has come to doubt whether cameras truly protect us, and instead put our privacy in jeopardy. This concern has been raised due to the internet's ever-growing presence and influence over technology, and conspiracy theories that government leaders are using the internet and CCTV cameras for surveillance.

MANHOLES

While we may sense and visualize the presence of the internet through different networks and forms of technology in front of us, it's ability to connect us to the world is made possible through cable networks established underground. This technology is responsible for sending and receiving data across communities, cities and countries. Though these coverings appear to be insignificant, they open the door to a vast networking system, designed to guarantee access to the web at your convenience.
